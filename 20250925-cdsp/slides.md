## FAIR metadata for Constances

- Constances is a large french health cohort
- Mekong: FAIR metadata with DDI Lifecycle as the center piece

Note:
~220 000 participants

----

- More or less well formed and complete source information (proto metadata)
- Need for standardizing and structuring but also to get more quality data
- Why not use AI/ML?

---

## Prototyping in 2024

- In 2024, two prototypes
- Kilimandjaro: matching words with terminologies entries using embeddings
- Varus: producing variable names from label using LLM chat request
- [Demo](https://github.com/Making-Sense-Info/Suivi-Constances/blob/main/ms13/livrables/ms13-p4/ms13-p4-p6.md)
